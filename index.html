What is Project: Empower?

Project: Empower is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization that brings students together to create software that empowers underprivileged communities. We are a team of high school and college students from across the world who are passionate about making a difference through technology.

We provide the resources to bring socially impactful software from imagination to reality

1

Project Initialization

Propose your idea to us or join one of our existing initiatives and we'll connect you with mentors and teams to work alongside you
 

2

Development Phase

Gain volunteer hours towards the President's Volunteer Service Award while working with our multi-talented network of students to develop projects from start to finish

3

Deployment

Our team will help completed projects gain visibility and extend their impact in the real world by networking with relevant communities
